Homestead Interact

Welcome To HHS Interact

Looking for a tight knit community to build lifelong friendships, earn community service hours, and most importantly have fun? Well, you're in the right place!

Interact is a service based club that focuses on two service projects- the Community Project and the International Project. Our members strive to raise as much money for these projects through attending volunteer events and meetings!

Community + International Projects

2018 - 2019

CPC: Future in Focus. Providing glasses and checkups for those who cannot afford it.

IPC: Se Necesita un Pueblo. Supporting the vulnerable youth of Venezuela with the opportunity for education, health, care, homes, and overall support.

2019 - 2020

CPC: Recovery Through Resilience. Increasing access to treatment for substance abuse, developing more efficient recovery programs, and destigmatizing conversations surrounding addiction.

IPC: Build for Tomorrow. Building schools in Nicaragua to reduce the #1 cause of poverty, a lack of education.

2020 - 2021

CPC: Combatting with Care. Giving health, education, and financial stability to those affected by the COVID-19 pandemic.

IPC: Feeding the Future. Relieving food insecurity for the citizens of Yemen.
